# Vision

## One-line Pitch

**Sigil** is a tactical card-driven dungeon crawler where your loadout *is* your deck.

## Product Goal

Build a highly replayable tactical RPG with satisfying build expression:

1. Equip items
2. Items grant cards
3. Cards define tactical options in combat
4. Combat outcomes influence loot and build evolution

## Design Goals

- Tactical clarity over chaos
- Strong item/card identity
- Replayable runs via procedural environments
- Deterministic combat simulation for testing and balancing


## Progression Principle

- **Rank-up progression** is a core identity layer: levels provide incremental growth, while rank-ups provide major build-defining choices.
