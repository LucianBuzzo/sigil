# Setting (LitRPG Foundation)

## World: Bastion

The game world is called **Bastion**.

## LitRPG Rule

In Bastion, game-like systems are explicit social reality:

- Levels are visible and discussed openly
- Classes and subclass paths are common knowledge
- Stats (core and derived) are known and interpreted culturally
- Build guides, archetypes, and “meta” discourse exist in-world
- Rank-ups are visible and socially meaningful milestones
- Rank paths and mutation choices are discussed publicly like class/build strategy

## Tone

- Arcane-tactical fantasy
- System-literate society
- Competence fantasy with meaningful constraints
